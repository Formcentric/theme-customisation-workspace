# Formcentric Theme Customisation Workspace

In the Formcentric Theme Customisation Workspace, you will find a toolkit that provides all
of the tools you need to customise Formcentric themes. You can either modify existing
themes or create your own variants. You can adjust theme variables, SCSS partials and
JavaScript templates to configure the design, markup and styling of your forms to match
your needs. Integrate your own fonts and images, and export your customisations for various
target environments.

## Why use this workspace?

- **Flexible**: make your changes at the level that makes sense for your needs.
- **Maintenance-friendly**: use a structured approach to theme development.
- **Professional**: build on top of official Formcentric themes.
- **Efficient**: live preview and hot reloading to accelerate development.
